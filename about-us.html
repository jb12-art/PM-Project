About us

Accessibility:

The museum is wheelchair-accessible and offers audio guides and braille displays for visually impaired visitors.

Café & Shop:

Enjoy light meals, coffee, and snacks at our museum café, and take home science-themed gifts and activity kits from our shop.

Explore Together at the Community Science Museum:

At the Community Science Museum, we are passionate about making science accessible, exciting, and interactive for everyone. Our mission is to spark curiosity, inspire discovery, and create a space where people of all ages can explore the wonders of the world around them.

From the origins of life to the vastness of space, from medical breakthroughs to artificial intelligence, we bring science to life through engaging exhibits, hands-on activities, and immersive educational programs. Whether you're a curious child, an educator looking to inspire students, or a researcher eager to explore new frontiers, our museum offers something for everyone.

What You’ll Discover:

Cosmology & Space Exploration – Journey through the cosmos in The Sky Above Us exhibit, where you can trace the stars, navigate galaxies, and follow the history of space exploration.

Evolution & Prehistoric Life – Step back in time to meet our Woolly Mammoth, explore the fascinating journey of evolution, and learn about species that once roamed the Earth.

Biology & Medicine – Discover the building blocks of life, explore groundbreaking medical advancements, and learn how science continues to improve our health and longevity.

Robotics & AI – The future is now! Interact with cutting-edge technology, see robotics in action, and even shake hands with Rob the Robot.

Ecology & Sustainability – Understand the impact of climate change, explore renewable energy solutions, and learn how we can work towards a sustainable future.

For Visitors of All Ages:

For Kids & Families – From interactive physics experiments to time-traveling exhibits, young explorers can learn while having fun! Join our Young Stars Club every Saturday for hands-on science experiments, or take part in our themed holiday camps.

For Educators & Schools – We offer guided tours, engaging workshops, and educational resources designed to inspire students and enhance learning beyond the classroom. Teachers can book field trips and access tailored science programs.

For Researchers & Academics – Our museum provides access to laboratory space, extensive archives, and collaboration opportunities with universities and scientific institutions.

Special Events & Exhibitions:

Guest Lectures & Expert Talks – Learn from leading scientists, including our upcoming lecture series with Professor Sheila Widnall on the future of aeronautics.

Night at the Museum – Experience science like never before with an overnight stay among the dinosaurs and exhibits. Bring your sleeping bag and sense of adventure!

Energetica Exhibition (On Loan from NEMO Science Museum) – A hands-on exploration of renewable energy, from wind turbines to solar power.

Join Us in Exploring the Wonders of Science!

Whether you’re here to learn, teach, research, or simply have fun, the Community Science Museum is a place where curiosity comes to life. Come and explore with us!
