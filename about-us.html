About us

Explore Together at the Community Science Museum

At the Community Science Museum, we believe that science should be accessible, engaging, and inspiring for everyone. Our mission is to create a space where curiosity is ignited, discoveries are made, and the wonders of science are brought to life. Whether you’re a budding scientist, an educator, or simply someone with a passion for learning, our museum offers something for all ages and interests.

Science has been a part of human history for thousands of years, shaping our understanding of the world—from the four elements of fire, wind, water, and earth to the vast galaxies that stretch across the universe. We celebrate this journey by offering interactive exhibits, immersive experiences, and hands-on learning opportunities designed to spark curiosity and wonder.

Our museum houses over 1,000 exhibits covering a wide range of scientific disciplines. Visitors can explore the cosmos, uncover the mysteries of evolution, dive into the world of biology and medicine, and even interact with the latest advancements in robotics and artificial intelligence. Our interactive exhibits ensure that science is not just something read about in textbooks, but something experienced firsthand.

We are committed to fostering a love for science in young minds through our engaging programs, such as the Young Stars club, school partnerships, and holiday camps. For educators, we provide guided tours, workshops, and resources to enhance classroom learning. For researchers, we offer access to laboratory space, academic collaborations, and digital archives to further scientific inquiry.

Beyond our permanent exhibitions, we host special events, visiting lectures, and traveling exhibitions from renowned science institutions worldwide. Our community-driven approach ensures that we are always evolving, staying at the forefront of scientific education and discovery.

Located in the heart of Oslo, the Community Science Museum is open to all, with free admission ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to explore and learn. Whether you’re here to marvel at the stars, uncover the secrets of ancient creatures, or witness the power of renewable energy, we welcome you to embark on a journey of discovery with us.

For Visitors of All Ages

For Kids & Families – From interactive physics experiments to time-traveling exhibits, young explorers can learn while having fun! Join our Young Stars Club every Saturday for hands-on science experiments, or take part in our themed holiday camps.

For Educators & Schools – We offer guided tours, engaging workshops, and educational resources designed to inspire students and enhance learning beyond the classroom. Teachers can book field trips and access tailored science programs.

For Researchers & Academics – Our museum provides access to laboratory space, extensive archives, and collaboration opportunities with universities and scientific institutions.

Accessibility

The museum is wheelchair-accessible and offers audio guides and braille displays for visually impaired visitors.

Café & Shop

Enjoy light meals, coffee, and snacks at our museum café, and take home science-themed gifts and activity kits from our shop.
